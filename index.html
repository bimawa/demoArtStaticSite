About

I create contemporary visual narratives inspired by changing city textures, natural rhythms, and memory. My work combines layered materials and restrained color to explore movement, stillness, and emotional contrast.

Through each series, I focus on atmosphere and detail, inviting viewers to slow down and notice subtle relationships between form, light, and surface.
